Systematic reviews and meta-analysis

Decision in healthcare and policy depend on highest available evidence. Systematic review and meta analyses rank highest in the evidence hierachy in informing such decisions. over the last two three decade,use in different field of enquiries has rapidly grown in length and breadth .This course provide participants with the rudiment of condulting systematic reviews and meta-analysis, covering all stages of the systematic review process, including questions formulation study , identification, data extraction,quality appraisal metal-analysis and many more. Tutoring is participatory , with hands on practical sessions that allow participants to implement the skills learned. The course is suitable for post graduate students faculty members, and researchers from any field of research. We have taught the course in several institutions accross europe. Recently the course was thought via Zoom to a selection of scholars accross africa.
